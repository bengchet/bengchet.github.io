My Little Tech Blog

Write a bit here, write a bit there

I started working as a Electronics Design Engineer at Cytron Technologies Sdn Bhd since year 2015 after graduated from Universiti Sains Malaysia. I have been involving in designing education-based boards and PCBs, such as Arduino shield, modules etc. Besides, I also have experience in electronic prototype and firmware development with third-party organisations such as Atilze Digital and Toshiba Tec Malaysia Manufacturing.

Since year 2015, IoT (Internet of Things) has emerged slowly in Malaysia and I have been given exposure to this technology from the beginning. Now I have 3 year experiences and still being active and specialised in WiFi and LoRaWAN development. I also helped our customers to provide consultation, technical assistance and end-to-end IoT solutions. Besides that, I am also company’s trainer since year 2016 and have been conducting IoT training for more than 6 times, mostly at school institutions.

I am also a lover in web designing, server development and phone app development. Specialised in html, css and javascript, I am also have experience on NodeJS and Angular framework for web development and Ionic framework for phone app development.
